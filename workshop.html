Overview

3D Printing is growing globally at such an alarming rate, there's never been a better time to learn and get involved with 3D design and printing!

Step inside the design and maker community with our workshop of designing, modelling and 3D printing your very own creation which you can take home with you!

Corporate Workshop Overview:

- Industry and technology overview
- Introduction to 3D Printing
- Types and properties of 3D printing materials available
- Introduction to 3D design software and modelling
- Exporting designs for 3D Printing

In school Program

This will be an introduction program that will give students an opportunity to learn about CAD software, everything from navigating and learning baisc tools menu,building a simple object, how to import and export files and discussing the range of applications using CAD as a process of manufacturing and testing ideas.
This program will build a foundation on the basic principles of CAD and can serve as an excellent starting point for students to explore and learn at their own time and at their own pace.

FAQs

Who can attend this workshop ?

Anyone with an open mind and a basic knowledge of operating a computer. 3D printing can be applied to almost all fields of study such as but not limited to engineering, medical, architectural, fashion, food technology, jewellery design, art and manufacturing. This workshop will help you unlock new possibilities in your field.

Do I need some prior knowledge ?

Nope. This workshop is for beginners.

What should I bring ?

A laptop - to start designing in 3D; and a USB Mouse - makes designing in 3D easier.

Do I get a certificate ?

Yes. Certificate will be provided to each participants by end of the session.

What will be provided for the workshop ?

A notebook and pen will be provided for you to take notes, if required. All software and other materials will be provided during the workshop.
