The Concept of a 3D Printer Lesson Plan

Your 3D lesson plans can be as simple or as complex as you choose. Only you can know the level of your students and their current ability.

What you decide will depend on the age level and ability of your class. The point is to keep new plans simple for new learners, and then adapt them as you go along if need be. As students, at any level, get to see their designs come to life, yours will be one of their favourite classes.
