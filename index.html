About us!

MyLeap is a young business lead by a group highly motivated and enthusiastic people who more and more got convinced of the 3D technique and its possibilities. Now is the time to do the job!

Our focus is on informing, teaching and sharing knowledge. We’re aiming mainly on primary and secondary schools where 3D printing is still applied only occasionally. This is what we’ld like to chance!

Adapting this technology into the classroom can renew, support, and perhaps even improve better understanding of subjects we’re in action to design suitable education. We are looking for co-creative partnerships with schools and offer innovative curricula.

MyLeap company aim is to offer and support implementation of leading educational programs (curricula) for primary and secondary education, bringing technology back to the classroom.

MyLeap only cooperate with professional parties and institutions that recognize the educational interest and like to contribute.
