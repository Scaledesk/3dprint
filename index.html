About us!

MyLeap is a young business lead by a group highly motivated and enthusiastic people who more and more got convinced of the 3D technique and its possibilities. Now is the time to do the job!

Our focus is on informing, teaching and sharing knowledge. We’re aiming mainly on primary and secondary schools where 3D printing is still applied only occasionally. This is what we’ld like to chance!

3D Printing Project Based Learning Modules

We believe our curricula and projects can and will excite students. We also know that not every school have teachers with technical know-how, which is why, we offer pre-training projects to the teachers involved prior to the start a project, and If so desired, we can also provide extended training and explanations to the entire school-team.

3D Lab For School

3D Lab, consists of a 3D Printer, design software in individual laptops / Computers, Filaments rolls stacked on the table, the 3D modules stored in a hard disk, a perfect trainer instructing on the model preparation.

This is the first such offering of its kind in the world making your school, a truly unique 21st century learning enabler.
